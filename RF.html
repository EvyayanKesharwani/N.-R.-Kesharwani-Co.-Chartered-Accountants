What is ROC Filing?

ROC (Registrar of Companies) Filing is a legal obligation for all companies registered in India. It involves submitting various annual returns and financial documents to the Ministry of Corporate Affairs to ensure legal compliance and corporate transparency.

How We Provide ROC Filing

At N. R. Kesharwani & Co., we assist in preparing and submitting all required ROC forms such as MGT-7 and AOC-4. We ensure that your company’s compliance calendar is up to date and help maintain proper records to avoid penalties or legal issues.

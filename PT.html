What is PT Registration (PTEC & PTRC)?

PT Registration refers to Professional Tax Registration. PTEC (Professional Tax Enrollment Certificate) is for individuals like professionals, consultants, and freelancers. PTRC (Professional Tax Registration Certificate) is for employers who deduct professional tax from employees’ salaries. Both are mandatory under the Maharashtra State Tax on Professions.

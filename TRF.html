What is TDS Return Filing?

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) Return Filing is the process of submitting quarterly statements to the Income Tax Department that report all TDS deducted and deposited by an organization. It ensures transparency and tracks the tax deducted from various payments such as salary, interest, rent, professional fees, and more.

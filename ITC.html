What is Import Export Code (IEC)?

IEC is a 10-digit code issued by the Director General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), required by anyone who intends to start import or export business in India. It acts as the key identifier for a business involved in international trade and is mandatory for customs clearance of shipments.

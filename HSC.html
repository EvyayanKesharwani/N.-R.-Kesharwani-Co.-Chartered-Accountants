What is Handling Scrutiny Cases?

Scrutiny cases refer to in-depth assessments carried out by the Income Tax Department when a return is selected for detailed review. Handling such cases involves representing the assessee before tax authorities, submitting necessary documentation, clarifying discrepancies, and responding to notices effectively. It ensures that your position is clearly presented and legally defended during assessment proceedings.

How We Handle Scrutiny Cases

At N. R. Kesharwani & Co., we handle scrutiny cases by first reviewing the notice, analyzing your financial documents, and identifying key areas the department may question. We then prepare and file detailed responses, attend hearings, and maintain correspondence with tax officers on your behalf. Our firm ensures compliance while safeguarding your rights and minimizing any potential liabilities.

What is Service Tax Return Filing?

Service Tax Return Filing is a legal requirement for service providers who fall under the scope of the erstwhile service tax laws. Even though GST has replaced service tax, there are still legacy cases where service tax returns need to be filed or revised. Filing ensures compliance with regulations and avoids penalties.

How We Provide Service Tax Return Filing

N. R. Kesharwani & Co. helps in reviewing your historical records, ensuring accurate computation of service tax liability, and filing or revising returns with the appropriate authorities. We also assist in resolving any notices or queries from the tax department.

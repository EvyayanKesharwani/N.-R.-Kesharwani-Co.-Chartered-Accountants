What is Preparation of Books of Account?

Preparation of Books of Account involves systematic recording, organizing, and summarizing of financial transactions of a business. It ensures proper documentation of income, expenses, assets, and liabilities, which is essential for compliance, taxation, auditing, and decision-making.

How We Provide this Service

At N. R. Kesharwani & Co., we use reliable accounting software and manual methods to maintain accurate books for your business. We handle ledger maintenance, bank reconciliations, journal entries, cash flow tracking, and inventory records, ensuring everything aligns with statutory requirements.

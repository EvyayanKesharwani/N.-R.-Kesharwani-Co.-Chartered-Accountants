What is Income Tax Matters Consultancy?

Income Tax Matters Consultancy is a professional service that offers support and advice in managing all aspects related to income tax. This includes filing returns, calculating liabilities, availing deductions, handling notices, rectifications, and compliance. We help individuals, businesses, and corporations remain compliant while optimizing their tax outcomes and resolving any issues that arise with tax authorities.

How We Provide Income Tax Services

At N. R. Kesharwani & Co., our process starts with understanding your financials and tax history. We offer personalized assistance in preparing accurate income tax returns, responding to notices, and managing advance tax payments. Our consultancy also includes strategic tax planning, representation before tax officers, and resolving pending tax disputes. You can count on us for end-to-end guidance throughout the fiscal year.

What is Audit of Books of Account?

Auditing the books of account involves the independent examination of financial records and statements to ensure accuracy, compliance with regulations, and the fair representation of a company’s financial position. It enhances the credibility of financial information presented to stakeholders.

How We Provide Audit Services

At N. R. Kesharwani & Co., we conduct thorough audits using modern techniques and a risk-based approach. We examine ledgers, journals, vouchers, and other financial documentation, identify discrepancies, suggest corrective actions, and ensure compliance with statutory standards and internal policies.

What is PAN & TAN Application?

PAN (Permanent Account Number) and TAN (Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number) are unique identifiers issued by the Income Tax Department of India. PAN is used for tracking financial transactions and ensuring tax compliance, while TAN is required for businesses or individuals who deduct or collect tax at source.
